It is a website like a rescue personnel that registers Volunteers (Professionals, Amateurs) in a disaster stuck situation. It performs commissioning of drones or humans as per requirement of the location. It accesses locations on basis of the calamity affect (Drones to monitor the locations). Drones are deployed to provide food or medicine if humans cannot reach or to access if rescue is required. Humans are deployed if rescue operation is required. Humans may have skills like Swimmers, Psychiatrist, Doctors, Nurse, Knowledge of first aid, etc.
